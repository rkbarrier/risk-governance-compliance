# Enterprise Audit Readiness Checklist

## Executive Overview

This Enterprise Audit Readiness Checklist provides a structured framework for preparing business and technology teams for internal and external audits. It helps verify that governance processes, documentation, controls, and evidence are complete, accurate, and readily available to support audit activities.

> **Portfolio Note:** This document is an original example created for professional portfolio purposes. It demonstrates enterprise audit readiness concepts and does not contain confidential or proprietary employer information.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this checklist is to:

* Improve audit preparedness.
* Verify governance documentation.
* Confirm control effectiveness.
* Support regulatory compliance.
* Reduce audit findings.
* Promote continuous improvement.

# Audit Evidence

Ensure the following documentation is available:

* Policies and standards
* Risk assessments
* Control testing results
* Governance committee meeting minutes
* Change management approvals
* Business continuity documentation
* Disaster recovery test results
* Executive reporting packages

---

# Executive Readiness Review

Before the audit begins, confirm:

* Executive stakeholders have been briefed.
* Requested documentation is organized.
* Control owners understand their responsibilities.
* Evidence repositories are complete.
* Open issues are documented with action plans.

---

# Success Measures

* Audit requests fulfilled on time.
* Documentation complete and accurate.
* Minimal audit findings.
* Timely remediation of observations.
* Continuous improvement actions tracked.
