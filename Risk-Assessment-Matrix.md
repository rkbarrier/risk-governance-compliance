# Technology Risk Assessment Matrix

## Executive Overview

This Technology Risk Assessment Matrix demonstrates a structured approach to identifying, assessing, and managing risks associated with an enterprise payment modernization initiative. It illustrates how business, technology, operations, and compliance teams collaborate to reduce operational risk while supporting secure, reliable, and compliant payment services.

> **Portfolio Note:** This document is an original example created for professional portfolio purposes. It is inspired by enterprise governance and risk management practices and does not contain confidential or proprietary employer information.

---

# Assessment Purpose

The objective of this assessment is to:

* Identify technology and operational risks.
* Evaluate the likelihood and business impact of each risk.
* Document mitigation strategies.
* Support governance and executive decision-making.
* Improve operational resilience.

# Risk Evaluation Criteria

### Likelihood

* Low – Unlikely to occur.
* Medium – May occur under certain conditions.
* High – Expected to occur without mitigation.

### Business Impact

* Low – Minimal operational impact.
* Medium – Noticeable disruption requiring management attention.
* High – Significant customer, financial, or regulatory impact.

---

# Top Priority Risks

## Payment Processing Availability

Continuous availability of payment systems is essential to support customer transactions, maintain regulatory compliance, and protect organizational reputation.

## Third-Party Dependencies

Critical payment services often rely on external providers. Regular resiliency reviews, service-level monitoring, and contingency planning help reduce vendor-related risks.

## Cybersecurity

Protecting payment platforms requires layered security controls, continuous monitoring, privileged access management, and rapid incident response capabilities.
